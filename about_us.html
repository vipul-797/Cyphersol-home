About Us

CYPHERSOL FINTECH INDIA PVT LTD

CypherSol has developed a state-of-the-art bank statement analyzer, which empowers organizations to extract structured insights and valuable information from their financial statement. Our clients spent hours analyzing large number of data transaction from multiple banks or multiple statements. We help them do this efficiently and in minimal time. CypherSol's solutions can be used by Tax Consultants, Direct Selling Agents, Financers and businesses to filter data-driven insights customized for their requirement. It offers business partners Safe, Accurate, Fast and Efficient analysis so they may better serve their clients. CypherSOL is a startup which was conceived by a team that has hands on experience in filtering loan applications and a Chartered Accountant. The board is being advised by a reputed BFSI analyst who spent 25 years covering financial sector. The team has used their practical experience to develop sophisticated tool that can simplify and save time for tax consultants for filing clients returns, financiers and DSA for assessing loan applications, and for businesses to filter transaction information.
